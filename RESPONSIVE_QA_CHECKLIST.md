# Responsive QA Checklist

Test these routes at: `360x800`, `390x844`, `768x1024`, `1280x800`.

## Layout
- Header controls keep minimum 44x44 touch area.
- Mobile menu closes on route change.
- No horizontal overflow in nav, content, or footer.

## Dashboard
- Stat cards stay readable and do not clip values.
- Recent orders stack correctly on mobile.
- Badge cards avoid text overflow and truncation issues.

## Closet
- Search, filters, and view toggle stack cleanly on small screens.
- List view stacks image, details, and actions vertically on narrow devices.
- Buttons remain fully visible and tappable.

## Marketplace
- Controls collapse to one-column group on mobile.
- Cards maintain aspect ratio and trade badges wrap without clipping.
- "Make Offer" remains visible with no overlap.

## Trade
- Trade target card stacks image and metadata on mobile.
- Trade status uses vertical timeline on mobile and horizontal on desktop.
- Submit action and demo controls remain reachable without overlap.
